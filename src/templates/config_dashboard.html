Live Trader

An advanced zero touch options engine that automates the full lifecycle of a short strangle: entry, stop loss placement, re entry after SL hits, and continuous delta based rebalancing. The strategy monitors option Greeks and price action in real time to dynamically exit or adjust legs when delta drifts beyond thresholds, restoring a balanced strangle profile without manual intervention. Designed for intraday and short term traders, it turns complex risk management—like leg selection, SL logic, and adjustment timing—into an end to end automated workflow so the trader only defines risk parameters and lets the system handle execution.
